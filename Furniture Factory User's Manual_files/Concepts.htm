Concepts

 

This section provides definition for some concepts that maybe not known to a regular player. Most of these are concepts related to the Industrial Engineering area that the game seeks to introduce.

 

·         Unit Load - A unit load combines individual items or items in shipping containers into single "units" that can be moved easily with a pallet jack or forklift truck. A unit load packs tightly into warehouse racks, intermodal containers, trucks, and boxcars, yet can be easily broken apart at a distribution point, usually a distribution center, wholesaler, retail store, etc.

 

·         Reorder Point - The reorder point ("ROP") is the level of inventory when an order should be made with suppliers to bring the inventory up by the Economic order quantity ("EOQ").

 

·         Process Flowchart - The flow process chart in industrial engineering is a graphical and symbolic representation of the processing activities performed on the work piece.

 

·         Operation – Is a process that changes the physical characteristics of the material in a Process Flowchart.

 

·         Activity – Is a processing step, usually denoted as a rectangular box in a Process Flowchart
